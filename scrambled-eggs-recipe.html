Scrambled Eggs

Looking for a good egg recipe? This one right here is the right way to scramble eggs. There is more to just mixing eggs and cooking! This will make a believer out of you.

prep time

5 min.

cook time

5 min.

serving

1 servings

Tags : breakfast Lunch dinner

instructions

step 1

Whisk together eggs, mayonnaise, and water in a cup or small bowl using a fork.

step 2

Melt margarine in a skillet over low heat. Pour in eggs and constantly stir as they cook. Remove eggs to a plate when they are set but still moist. Do not overcook. Season with salt and pepper to taste once eggs are on the plate.

ingredients

2 large eggs

1 teaspoon mayonnaise or salad dressing

1 teaspoon water (Optional)

1 teaspoon margarine or butter

salt and pepper to taste
